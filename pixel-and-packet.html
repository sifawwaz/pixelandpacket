How We Work

From idea to live site in under 2 weeks.

01

About Us

Two IT students who decided to build something.

We're Fawwaz and a friend — both studying IT and Networking at Ontario Tech University in the Greater Toronto Area. We got tired of seeing websites that look beautiful but load slowly, break on mobile, or cost a fortune to maintain.

So we started Pixel & Packet. We combine design taste with real technical knowledge — the kind that only comes from studying networking, security, and systems. We know why performance matters, and we build it in from the start.

We work with students who need portfolios, local businesses who need a real web presence, and anyone who wants a site that actually works.
